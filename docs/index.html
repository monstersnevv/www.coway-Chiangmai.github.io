    A history of home-health technology

    At Coway, wellness is at the heart of our business philosophy. Since our founding in 1989, Coway has had one overriding mission: to help people live healthier, happier lives. As an award-winning Korean home products company with an emphasis on engineering excellence, design thinking and user experience, we create innovative technologies to promote health in the home. Now we are bringing our expertise to North America with leading air purifier, water purifier and bidet solutions, and continue to focus on our passion. We are creating a better world with state-of-the-art products and services, by combining efficiency and modern design to deliver the best home wellness solutions.
